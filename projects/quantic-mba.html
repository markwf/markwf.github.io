Quantic MBA


The world’s first elite online MBA.

I completed the world’s first mobile MBA; taught with a revolutionary method that is fast and proven effective by independent researchers from Stanford. In both core subjects tested, Quantic (formerly Smartly) rated as effective as a top 10 MBA classroom.


Why did I choose Quantic?

The MBA program is an elite online program that offers a free MBA to the best & brightest. The rigorous curriculum consists of courses from nine subject areas, including accounting, finance, marketing, and other core areas of business education. However, Quantic is different from a traditional MBA in the sense that our instructional method is completely digital, and you can complete the program on any device. There are no lectures or PowerPoints, and students interact with the platform every 8.7 seconds on average. Thus, you can earn your degree concurrently with a full-time job or another degree.

Quantic's MBA also ties the education directly into career development. Upon enrolling into the program, students join Quantic Talent, which is an alumni and employer network that helps Quantic students connect with each other and discover new job opportunities.

How competitive is the program?

Thousands of applicants apply to the Quantic MBA each round. Over 80% of their students went to top-20 undergraduate and graduate schools in their fields and the average GMAT score of students is over 700. The main criteria for admission are:

  • The selectivity and academic rigor of the applicant's undergraduate institution
  • Competitiveness of the applicant's GPA
  • Depth and quality of work experience



What do MBA students learn?

Aligned with the world's leading business schools, the MBA degree comprises courses across business disciplines. Quantic's program consists of nine subject areas, including Business Foundations, Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Economics, Business Strategy, Data and Analysis, Leadership and Management, Supply Chain and Operations.

Who developed the courses?

Quantic's talented team of content creators, editors, and subject-matter experts, including professors from elite institutions, builds each course with smart, motivated learners in mind to ensure that the courses meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving business environment. Quantic lessons are bite-sized and interactive, crafted using their proprietary learning platform. Quantic is expanding the possibilities of online education.

How effective is Quantic?

The Quantic program has been validated through formal research. In an independent academic study, Quantic students performed as well or better than MBA students from HBS, Stanford, Wharton, and Fuqua in a test of accounting, and performed as well as the MBA students in a test of finance. While there are benefits to a traditional physical school including in-person interactions among professors and students and collaborative assignments between students, Quantic is proud that the quality of the instruction rivals that of the top-10 business schools worldwide.

Is Quantic accredited?

Yes, Quantic School of Business and Technology is licensed by the Office of the State Superintendent of Education (OSSE) in Washington, DC and accredited by the Distance Education Accreditation Commission (DEAC). The DEAC is listed by the United States Department of Education as a recognized accrediting agency and is also recognized by the Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).
